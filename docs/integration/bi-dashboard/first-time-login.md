1. Go to [SQL Business Intelligence Dashboard](https://bi.sql.com.my/), click on **Need an account?**

   ![Need an account](../../../static/img/integration/bi-dashboard/first-time-login/1.png)

2. Enter your registered email with SQL, click on send verification email

   ![Send verification email](../../../static/img/integration/bi-dashboard/first-time-login/2.png)

3. Check your mailbox, and click on "Sign in to SQL B.I Dashboard"

   ![Check mailbox](../../../static/img/integration/bi-dashboard/first-time-login/3.png)

4. Enter your registered email and click sign in

   ![Sign in](../../../static/img/integration/bi-dashboard/first-time-login/4.png)

5. Next, enter your name and click save

   ![Enter name](../../../static/img/integration/bi-dashboard/first-time-login/5.png)

6. Enter your password, repeat password & click save

   ![Enter password](../../../static/img/integration/bi-dashboard/first-time-login/6.png)

7. Read and click agree the term of use

   ![Term of use](../../../static/img/integration/bi-dashboard/first-time-login/7.png)

8. Read and click agree the privacy policy

   ![Privacy policy](../../../static/img/integration/bi-dashboard/first-time-login/8.png)

9. Login to dashboard

   ![Login to dashboard](../../../static/img/integration/bi-dashboard/first-time-login/9.png)
